>>> Kyle Parker Cunningham

Story telling is art. I do both. I also adventure, often. This website is the amalgamation of my art. Some of it is straightforward, much of it is not. This page tries to be as simple as possible.

I'm primarily an oil painter interested in a wide variety of subject matter. My work is about time and memory and an imagined future. Together, it tells the story which is my own existence and the adventures and experiences I've felt and then twisted up and made into a brand new story.
